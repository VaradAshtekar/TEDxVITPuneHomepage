COVID Specifications

What COVID precautionary measures have been taken for the event?

All the government regulations regarding COVID-19 will be carried out. The entire organizing team will undergo COVID test before the event, in order to increase the safety of the attendees. Packaged food and beverages will be provided at the venue. All attendees, speakers, team members and working staff will be required to wear masks at all times and follow social distancing at all locations.

What measures have been taken regarding social distancing at the event?

The event venue has a seating capacity of 800, wherein 100 attendees can easily be accommodated. Alternate seating protocols will be followed. Social Distancing mandates will be followed in each activity zone.

What measures have been taken with regards to air conditioning within the venue?

Guidelines given by the Government of India will be followed. Temperature setting of all air conditioning devices would range between 24°C and 30°C. Relative Humidity will range between 40 to 70%.

Will any medical proof be needed while entering the event venue (COVID-19)?

No medical proof is needed, however basic precautionary checking which includes temperature check, oxygen saturation and sanitization will be done before entering. Any attendee found to have COVID like symptoms will not be permitted inside. TEDxVITPune reserves the right to deny entry to any attendee under such circumstances.

Will TEDxVITPune be providing any COVID Safety kits?

Every Attendee will be provided with masks and sanitizers in their Merchandise Bag. Wearing a mask is mandatory at all times. Sanitizer dispensers will be available at all public places.

How will food and beverages be served at the event?

Complete precautions will be taken in the distribution of food and beverages. Package food and beverages will be provided to the attendees in a contactless manner. Multiple collection points will be set up to maintain social distancing.
